Instagram Followers Check

Follow these steps to download and upload your Instagram followers and following files:

  1. Go to your Instagram profile page, press the top right corner three-line logo, go to settings and privacy.
  2. Go to Accounts Centre -> Your information and permissions -> Download your information -> Download or transfer information -> Some of your information
  3. Select only Followers and following under Connections, press next when done.
  4. Select Download to device and press next, choose All time for data range, enter the email you want to receive the files in Notify, choose HTML for Format. Media quality doesnâ€™t matter as you are not downloading images or videos. Choose Create files when done.
  5. It will take some time for the file to be sent to your email, 1 minute to 1 day. When you receive the email, download it and upload the respective followers and following file and press upload files.
  6. The list of your Following that are not following you back is now generated.
